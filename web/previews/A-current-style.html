Frequently Asked Questions

What is a CHIP?

A CHIP (Credit Hour In Pool) is a proposed unit for measuring value, indexed to one hour of unskilled human labor in a balanced global market.

How is the CHIP value calculated?

Using econometric models applied to panel data from dozens of countries, estimating the marginal product of unskilled labor and adjusting for market distortions.

How can I get involved in CHIP research?

Contact us if you're interested in contributing research. Funding may be available for bona fide research participants.
